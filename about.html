Our Story

Founded in the heart of Nairobi, Amalia Nutritive Supplies was born from a passion for natural wellness and healthy living. We believe that good health starts with good nutrition, and that nature provides the best ingredients to fuel our bodies. Our mission is to make high-quality, natural foods and supplements accessible to our community.

From our physical store at Jamia Mall to our new online presence, we are dedicated to providing our customers with products they can trust. We carefully source our inventory, prioritizing organic, non-GMO, and sustainably produced goods. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to offer guidance and help you find the perfect products for your health journey.
